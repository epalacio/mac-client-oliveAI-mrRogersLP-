Olive optimizes and manages the healthcare supply chain

Supplies are the second highest spend for health systems — after salaries. Reducing supply chain variation can save hospitals up to $9.9 million per year.1 Olive manages supplies and inventory across your enterprise to reduce costs.

Use a data-driven sourcing strategy

Olive analyzes spending habits and measures the cost of goods. She proactively uses vendor-agnostic analytics to manage product portfolios and identify high-quality products at competitive prices.1

Refine materials management for greatest cost savings

Using intelligent automation, Olive connects your EHR and ERP to improve sourcing and materials management. Olive evaluates historical trends to make recommendations on physician preference card updates, item master cleanup and inventory management.

Streamline procurement and accounts payable

From initial purchase order to final payment, Olive double checks that all delivered supplies match the items purchased. Olive reviews and reconciles POs and inventory before routing invoices to the correct approvers and accounts payable teams.
